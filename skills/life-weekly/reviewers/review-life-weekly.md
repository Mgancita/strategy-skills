# Reviewer: Life Weekly Check-in

Review weekly check-ins for accomplishment tracking, monthly mapping, and realistic priorities.

**Purpose:** Verify last week is reviewed, monthly alignment is checked, and weekly priorities are actionable.
**Dispatch after:** Weekly check-in is drafted.

## What to Check

| Category | What to Look For |
|----------|------------------|
| Last Week Reviewed | Were last week's tasks addressed (done, carried, dropped)? Flag if skipped. |
| Monthly Mapping | Is there a check against monthly goals? Flag if weekly priorities are disconnected. |
| Off-Track Flagging | If something is drifting from monthly goals, is it called out? |
| Priority Count | 2-4 specific priorities. Flag if more than 4 or too vague. |
| Specificity | Are priorities concrete actions? "Be active" is vague. "Gym Monday and Wednesday" is good. |
| Brevity | Should be quick. Flag if it's become a long planning document. |

## Calibration

Weekly is practical. Not strategic, not reflective. Just: what happened, what's next, are we on track?

**Approve if:** Last week reviewed, monthly alignment checked, 2-4 specific priorities set.
**Revise if:** No review of last week, priorities disconnected from monthly goals, more than 4 items, or everything is vague.

## Output Format

**Status:** Approved | Issues Found

**Issues (if any):**
- [Section]: [specific issue] -- [why it matters]

**Recommendations (advisory):**
- [suggestions for improvement]
