# Reviewer: Life Monthly Check-in

Review the monthly life check-in for goal quality, progress tracking, and realistic planning.

**Purpose:** Verify wins are celebrated, TODOs are properly updated, and new goals are specific and realistic.
**Dispatch after:** Monthly check-in is drafted.

## What to Check

| Category | What to Look For |
|----------|------------------|
| Wins Acknowledged | Were wins celebrated before planning? Flag if the check-in jumps straight to goals. |
| Previous Goals Reviewed | Were last month's goals addressed (completed, carried, revised, dropped)? Flag if old goals are ignored. |
| TODO Hygiene | Are carried-forward items a conscious decision, not just silently moved? Flag ghost items. |
| Goal Count | 2-4 goals for next month. Flag if more than 4 or none at all. |
| Goal Specificity | Are goals specific and measurable? "Exercise more" is too vague. "Gym 3x/week" is good. |
| Realism | Is the total load realistic for one month? Flag if overloaded. |
| Vision Alignment | Do goals loosely connect to the life vision? Flag completely random goals with no connection. |
| Recurring Misses | If the same goal has been missed 3+ months in a row, flag it for rethinking. |

## Calibration

Monthly life check-ins should feel personal but actionable. More structured than a journal entry but less rigid than a business quarterly review.

**Approve if:** Wins acknowledged, old goals addressed, 2-4 new specific goals set.
**Revise if:** No wins section, old goals ignored, goals are all vague, or the list is overwhelming.

## Output Format

**Status:** Approved | Issues Found

**Issues (if any):**
- [Section]: [specific issue] -- [why it matters]

**Recommendations (advisory):**
- [suggestions for improvement]
