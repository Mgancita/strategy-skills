# Business & Life Strategy Plugin

A strategy system split into two tracks: **Business** for company/project planning, and **Life** for personal reflection and growth. Each track has its own cadence and tone.

## Business Strategy

Structured planning that connects your business vision to daily execution. Six skills cover every cadence from annual review to daily standup.

Strategy files are saved in `strategy/` organized by year, quarter, month, and week. Every task traces back through outcomes, focus areas, and the business plan.

### Skills

- **business-initial** -- First-time setup. Build your Business Plan and Core Focus areas. ("I have a business idea", "help me plan my business")
- **business-yearly** -- Deep annual review of vision, focus, and outcomes. ("yearly review", "annual planning")
- **business-quarterly** -- Check business plan for shifts, set quarterly targets with pace tracking. ("quarterly check-in", "Q2 planning")
- **business-monthly** -- Set monthly outcomes and break them into project-level tasks. ("monthly check-in", "plan for this month")
- **business-weekly** -- Break monthly projects into weekly deliverables. ("plan my week", "weekly check-in")
- **business-daily** -- Fast 3-minute standup. Propose, confirm, done. ("what should I do today?", "morning check-in")

## Life Strategy

Reflective, personal planning across four areas: Health & Fitness, Relationships & Social, Personal Growth, and Finance & Lifestyle. Less structured than business. No forced metrics or KPIs.

Life strategy files are saved in `life-strategy/` with a simpler structure: vision file plus monthly and weekly reflections.

### Skills

- **life-initial** -- Explore what matters to you across life areas. Long-term dreams, medium-term progress, short-term starts. ("life planning", "what do I want in life", "personal goals")
- **life-monthly** -- Monthly reflection. Celebrate wins, acknowledge struggles, set 2-4 gentle intentions. ("life monthly check-in", "how's my life going")
- **life-weekly** -- Quick 10-minute check-in. One win, gentle monthly alignment, 1-3 priorities. ("life weekly check-in", "personal weekly review")
- **life-daily** -- 2-3 minute grounding moment. Mood, one intention, optional gratitude. ("life daily check-in", "how am I doing today")

## Key Difference Between Tracks

Business strategy is structured and metric-driven: SMART goals, pace tracking, alignment validation.

Life strategy is reflective and intention-driven: no forced metrics, warm tone, permission to skip areas, no judgment.

They are completely independent. Run them separately based on what you need.
